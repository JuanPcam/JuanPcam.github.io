Lionel Messi

  • Born in Rosario, Argentina, Lionel Messi is regarded as the greatest FC Barcelona player, and the greatest of all time.
  • At the age of 13, he joined Barcelona's academy from Newell's Old Boys' academy.
  • Mainly a right winger, Messi can play as a left winger, center-forward, second-striker and even as an attacking midfielder.
  • He spent 17 years at the club from 2003-2021, scoring 672 goals and providing 303 assists in 778 appearences.
  • Messi is known for his dribbling, finishing, positioning, incredible vision, and range of passing.
  • As his career developed, his tendency of dribbling slightly diminished with age, starting to dictate play in deeper areas of the pitch.This lead him to be regarded as one of the best passers and playmakers in history.
  • He won 35 titles with Barcelona:
  • 10 Spanish Championships.
  • 8 Spanish Super Cups.
  • 7 Spanish Cups.
  • 4 Champions League.
  • 3 UEFA Super Cup.
  • 3 FIFA Club World Cup.

Johan Cruyff

  • Johan Cruyff spent 5 years at Barcelona, from 1973-1978.
  • His main position was as an attacking mifielder, but he could also played as a center-forward, left or right winger, center midfielder, and even went on to play as a center-back.
  • He scored 60 goals and 8 assists for the club in 180 games.
  • Cruyff only managed to win 2 titles: a Spanish Championship and a Spanish Cup
  • Despite his normal stats, he became a Barcelona legend after helping the team win their first Championship in 14 years.
  • Cruyff later return to Barcelona, this time as a coach, leading the team to a glorious era.
  • He coached barcelona for 8 years from 1988-1996.
  • He went on to win 11 titles:
  • 4 Spanish Championships.
  • 3 Spanish Super Cups.
  • 1 Spanish Cup.
  • 1 Champions League.
  • 1 UEFA Super Cup.
  • 1 European Cup Winners' Cup.
  • During his time as a coach, Cruyff gave Barcelona an identity by their way of playing and use of the youth academy- La Masia.

Xavi Hernandez

  • Xavi Hernandez spent 17 years at FC Barcelona, from 1998-2015.
  • Xavi was born in Terrassa, Barcelona, and came from the academy of Barcelona.
  • Mainly a center midfielder, he could also play as a defensive or attacking midfielder, and even as a left or right midfielder.
  • He is regarded as the best midfielder of his generation, while also being considered one of the best midfielders of all time.
  • Xavi was a deep-lying playmaker, whose role was to find spaces and exploit them .
  • A composed, agile, and technically skilled player, Xavi is known for dictating the flow of the game, with his outstanding vision, pinpoint passing, excellent reading of the game, and world-class ball control.
  • Xavi went on to score 85 goals and provide 185 assists in 767 appearances .
  • He won 25 titles with Barcelona:
  • 8 Spanish Championships.
  • 6 Spanish Super Cups.
  • 4 Champions League.
  • 3 Spanish Cups.
  • 2 UEFA Super Cups.
  • 2 FIFA Club World Cup.
  • 6 years after he left the club, in November of 2021, Xavi returned to Barcelona as a coach.
  • He came to a Barcelona in crisis, who couldn't compete both domestically and in Europe.
  • He coached FC Barcelona for 3 years, from 2021-2024.
  • In his second year at the club, he managed to win the Spanish Championship, the first one after 4 years and the first one without Lionel Messi.
  • During 3 seasons, he also managed to win the Spanish Super Cup.

Andres Iniesta

  • Andres Iniesta was born in Fuentealbilla, Albacete.
  • He is from Barcelona's academy- La Masia.
  • Iniesta spent 16 years at the club from 2002-2018.
  • He scored 57 goals and provided 136 assists in 674 appearences.
  • Just like his former club-mate Xavi, Iniesta was also a playmaker, known for his technique, passing, intuition, create chances and space for teammates, and his dictation of the midfield and play.
  • Due to his balance, ball control and agility in small spaces, skill, composure, and flair on the ball, Iniesta could play more than just as a central midfielder but also as a defensive and attacking midfielder. Nonetheless, he could also play as a left midfielder, left or right winger, second-striker, and even as a center-forward.
  • Iniesta won 32 titles with Barcelona:
  • 9 Spanish Championships.
  • 7 Spanish Super Cups.
  • 6 Spanish Cups.
  • 4 Champions League.
  • 3 UEFA Super Cup.
  • 3 FIFA Club World Cup.

Gerard Pique

  • Gerard Pique was born in Barcelona, Catalonia.
  • He came trough the ranks of Barcelona's academy- La Masia.
  • Pique spent 15 years at the club from 2008-2023.
  • His main position was a center-back, but he could also play as a right-back, left-back, and even as a defensive midfielder.
  • Despite being a defender, Pique went on to score 53 goals and provide 13 assists in 640 appearances.
  • Pique was known for being a modern and commanding denfender, who combined strength and tackling ability with good technique and passing.
  • In 2019, he was regarded as the best defender in the world, due to his intelligence, leadership, positional sense, composure, and anticipation, and being good in the air due to his height and physical attributes.
  • During his time at Barcelona, he won 31 titles:
  • 9 Spanish Championships.
  • 7 Spanish Cups.
  • 6 Spanish Super Cups.
  • 3 Champions League.
  • 3 UEFA Super Cups.
  • 3 FIFA Club World Cups.
