Andres Iniesta

  • Andres Iniesta was born in Fuentealbilla, Albacete.
  • He is from Barcelona's academy- La Masia.
  • Iniesta spent 16 years at the club from 2002-2018.
  • He scored 57 goals and provided 136 assists in 674 appearences.
  • Just like his former club-mate Xavi, Iniesta was also a playmaker, known for his technique, passing, intuition, create chances and space for teammates, and his dictation of the midfield and play.
  • Due to his balance, ball control and agility in small spaces, skill, composure, and flair on the ball, Iniesta could play more than just as a central midfielder but also as a defensive and attacking midfielder. Nonetheless, he could also play as a left midfielder, left or right winger, second-striker, and even as a center-forward.
  • Iniesta won 32 titles with Barcelona:
  • 9 Spanish Championships.
  • 7 Spanish Super Cups.
  • 6 Spanish Cups.
  • 4 Champions League.
  • 3 UEFA Super Cup.
  • 3 FIFA Club World Cup.
